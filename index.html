Capabilities

01

Document Upload

Users can easily upload their identification documents (such as driver's license, passport, or ID card) directly through the website. This capability streamlines the KYC process by allowing users to submit their documents electronically, eliminating the need for physical document submission.

02

Image Capture

Users have the option to take a picture of themselves using their device's camera, which is then used for identity verification purposes. This feature enhances security by providing a real-time image for comparison and validation against the submitted documents.

03

OCR Text Comparison

The website utilizes Tesseract.js OCR technology to extract text from uploaded documents and images. This extracted text is then compared to the information entered by the user, ensuring accuracy and consistency in the verification process. This capability automates the verification process and minimizes the potential for manual errors.

04

Verification Feedback

Upon submission of documents and images, users receive prompt feedback regarding the status of their verification process. They are informed whether their documents have been successfully processed or if any additional steps are required. This capability enhances transparency and user satisfaction by keeping users informed throughout the verification journey.

Why KYC?

Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures are essential for businesses and financial institutions to comply with regulations, mitigate risks, and prevent financial crimes. By verifying the identities of their customers, organizations can adhere to legal requirements aimed at preventing money laundering, terrorism financing, and fraud. KYC also enables businesses to assess the level of risk associated with each customer, implement appropriate risk management strategies, and prevent illicit activities.

Moreover, robust KYC measures help maintain trust and reputation with customers and stakeholders by demonstrating a commitment to protecting their identities and financial information. Through enhanced customer due diligence, businesses can make informed decisions about providing services and managing customer relationships, contributing to the integrity of financial systems overall.
